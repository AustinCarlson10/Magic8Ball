About Us

All of Your Aquarium Needs

We can do it all. From simple fish only, to full blown reef tanks, we can help.

We have bred seahorses and cuttlefish, and built public aquarium exhibits for giant pacific octopus.

Allow your dreams to come true. Your only limitation is your imagination!

A Commitment to Healthy Specimens

All of our corals go through extensive dips, medication, and are constantly being manicured and groomed daily to ensure we always offer the best specimens in the hobby. Our fish are selected from only the best collectors, and upon entry into our facility, the fish are placed into holding systems with copper, they are fed high quality food and medications to ensure our customers get the healthiest specimens. We always recommend quarantine and dipping prior to adding to your display to ensure your investment is protected. Ask our specialists about our procedures and recommendations. We gladly share what has worked for us!

Customer Satisfaction

While we cannot guarantee all of our specimens will thrive in your aquarium, what we can guarantee is if you do lose something in a short time, come see us and we will do our best to make it right. We understand this hobby is expensive and things happen. Allow us to help you through your trials and errors, you are not alone. If your tank has a problem, we want to help! If your tank is happy, so are we.

Shipping Procedure and Guarantee

We have several years of shipping everything from sharks and stingrays, to octopus, and corals. We have always had the same guarantee, our animals will arrive in wonderful condition, triple bagged and rubber banded to ensure no leaky bags. We use 1" thick formed styrofoam boxes with an outside cardboard box to ensure there is minimal temperature fluctuation and is well protected from the outside weather. Depending on weather we may add heat packs or cold packs, but this is only in extreme weather. In the unfortunate time of a loss during shipping, we require an email with picture of the dead animal, within 2 hours of delivery stamp, and you will receive a full credit to be used for your next purchase. On any replacement, you only pay the shipping. In the unfortunate event there is a delay in the shipping company, we cannot be held responsible. We can offer another piece at an extreme discount, but unfortunately no shipping company at this time offers a full guarantee on overnight shipping. We ship on Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday for next day delivery by 2pm. If there is any problems with receiving your package by said time, immediately contact us and we will try our best to help facilitate any problems with the shipping company.
